About the Show

As the successor to the legendary BEANSMAS, Hallobean is a thrilling new object show that will make you scream... with laughter and excitement! Six unsuspecting contestants will compete in a series of bone-chilling challenges to win the coveted Golden Bean. From haunted house escapades to pumpkin carving contests, anything can happen in this frightfully fun festival of fear!

Meet the Host

The Bean Man

Your guide through this terrifying tournament is none other than the Bean Man himself! With a flair for the dramatic and a love for all things spooky, the Bean Man will keep the contestants on their toes and the audience on the edge of their seats.

The Contestants

Five brave (or foolish) objects have entered the competition, but only one can be crowned the Hallobean champion. Who will triumph, and who will be spirited away? Stay tuned to find out!

Bean-O-Lantern

With a fiery spirit and a glowing personality, Bean-O-Lantern is here to light up the competition!

Ghost Bean

Boo! Ghost Bean plans to float through the challenges, but will their bashful nature scare them out of the running?

Zombean

Slow, steady, and with a monstrous appetite for victory. Zombean is dying to win the Golden Bean.

Candy Beansket

Sweet on the outside, but full of tricks! Candy Beansket is a sugary strategist with a competitive core.

Skelebean

Skelebean has a bone to pick with the other contestants and is rattling with excitement to prove they have the backbone to win.
